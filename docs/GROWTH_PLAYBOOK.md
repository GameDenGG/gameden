# NEWWORLD Growth Playbook

This document outlines the **traffic, growth, and user acquisition
strategy** for the NEWWORLD Steam Deals platform.

The goal is to turn NEWWORLD into a major discovery engine for Steam
games by combining:

-   deal intelligence
-   SEO discovery pages
-   viral deal sharing
-   alerts and retention loops

This playbook focuses on **scalable growth loops** that compound over
time.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Core Growth Principle

NEWWORLD should not position itself as:

"A site that lists Steam discounts."

Instead it should be:

"The best place to discover which discounted Steam games are worth
buying right now."

This positioning combines:

-   price signals
-   player activity
-   review quality
-   momentum trends

These signals create a much stronger discovery engine than simple
discount tracking.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Primary Traffic Engine: SEO

SEO will be the largest long-term traffic source.

Pages should target **high intent search queries**.

Examples:

Top Steam Deals Today\
Best Steam Deals\
Steam Games on Sale\
Best Steam RPG Deals\
Best Steam Co-op Deals\
Best Steam Strategy Deals\
Best Steam Deals Under \$20\
Trending Steam Games\
New Historical Low Steam Deals

These pages should be:

-   server-rendered
-   regularly updated
-   internally linked
-   optimized for search intent

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Viral Deal Sharing

People share deals when they understand **why the deal matters**.

NEWWORLD's viral feature is the **Deal Heat Engine**.

Each deal should include a short explanation:

Examples:

"Players up 200% this week and now at historical low."

"Overwhelmingly Positive co-op hit discounted for the first time."

"Major update + weekend sale causing player surge."

These explanations create:

-   urgency
-   social proof
-   shareability

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Social Share Cards

Every deal page should generate shareable cards.

Cards should include:

Game title\
Game artwork\
Discount percent\
Current price\
Deal heat explanation

These cards make it easy to share deals on:

Reddit\
Discord\
Twitter/X\
Gaming forums

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Community Growth Loops

## Discord Communities

Deal hunters frequently share deals in gaming Discords.

Encourage sharing by:

-   making deal pages easy to copy
-   generating short summaries
-   adding share buttons

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Reddit

Subreddits like:

r/GameDeals\
r/Steam\
r/PCGaming\
r/IndieGaming

are major discovery hubs.

Deals with strong explanations and social proof perform best.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Retention Loops

Traffic alone is not enough.

NEWWORLD must convert visitors into returning users.

The primary retention features are:

Wishlist tracking\
Price alerts\
Historical low alerts\
Trending deal alerts

These notifications bring users back whenever deals change.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
